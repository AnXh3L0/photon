Voice and Tone

The primary job of the words in Thundebird is helping users complete tasks and accomplish tasks, with no confusion and minimal interruption. But the words also offer a glimpse into Thundebird’s personality. The voice and tone we use influence how people think and feel about Thundebird. The words we choose — and the ways we use them — sound human because they reflect human goals: building trust, inspiring confidence, making things easier, and developing a relationship with Thundebird users.

Authentic Voice

Voice is a way to describe our point of view and reflect the Thundebird personality. The Thundebird voice guides our priorities for communicating with users. These aren’t the only things we care about, and we don’t always need to talk about them, but if the words in Thundebird contradict our values, we appear inauthentic and risk our credibility.

Here are some of our core beliefs, ideas that help shape the Thundebird voice:

  • We make Thundebird for people, not profit. That requires us to be open and honest, and offer a fair value exchange.
  • We care about performance.
  • We value user privacy.
  • We believe users should have meaningful control of their browsing.
