Photon Design System

The Thunderbird Design System based on Firefox Photon.

Photon is the Firefox design language to build modern, intuitive, delightful experiences, for products across all platforms. For Thunderbird we use Photon as well, however tailored for Thunderbird specifically. That means that many patterns you will find in Photon for Firefox don't live here. The Thunderbird design system can be considered a fork of Firefox Photon.

You can help us improve the system and ensure it remains current and relevant. We suggest contributing to Firefox Photon and suggesting downstream adoptions here afterwards later on.
